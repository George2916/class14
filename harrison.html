Harrison Woodell Greenslade is born on February 15, 2002, the son of Steve and Lori Greenslade. He has two older sisters and an older brother. Famous people born on February 15th include singer/songwriter Melissa Manchester and actor Cesar Romero.

In April 2002, Uncle George meets Harrison at his initiation into the Catholic church.

Uncle George is asked to be Harrison's godfather(1), which automatically moves Harrison to first place in his uncle's list of favorite nephews.

In July 2002, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George meet Harrison and his family in Buffalo at his paternal grandparents' home. (2)(3)

In October 2002, Harrison and his family meet Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George in Westchester on the occasion of the entering into holy matrimony by Harrison's Uncle James and Aunt Stephanie.(4)

In February 2004, Harrison and his family meet many members of the Greenslade clan, including Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George, in Schenectady.(5)

In April 2004, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit Harrison and his family in Topsfield.(6)(7)

In March 2005, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit the Greenslades in Topsfield.(8)

In April 2006, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit the Greenslades in Topsfield.(9)

In April 2009, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit Harrison and his family in Topsfield.(10)

In September 2009, after attending the wedding of cousin Allison Duffy, the Greenslades of Topsfield and the Greenslades of New York City meet at Uncle Brian's house in New Jersey, along with many other relatives of Harrison on his paternal grandmother's side of the family.(11)

In December 2009, Harrison and his family meet Uncle George and his family and others in Myrtle Beach to celebrate the 50th wedding anniversary of HW's paternal grandparents.(12)Immediately after this picture is taken, Harrison has to be carried by his paterfamilias to the bathroom in order to hurl and that is why neither of them is in the picture from December 2009 on Aidan's page.

In February 2010, Harrison visits New York City with his siblings and paterfamilias. We stop in Economy Candy on Rivington Street(13), which appears to delight Harrison and his siblings.

In April 2011, Harrison visits New York City with his siblings and his paterfamilias.(14)

In March 2013, Uncle George visits the Greenslades in Topsfield. Harrison and Aidan take on Uncle George in a two-on-one tackle football game in the snow and lose resoundingly.

In May 2013, Uncle George visits the Greenslades in Topsfield.(15)(16)

In May 2015, Uncle George visits Topsfield.(17)

In November 2015, Harrison visits the Greenslades in New York City with Uncle Ken, cousin Colin and brother Aidan.(18)
