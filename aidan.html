Aidan Charles Greenslade is born on April 20, 2000, the son of Steve and Lori Greenslade. He has two older sisters, Haley and Abigail. Famous people born on April 20th include Adolf Hitler and Steve Spurrier.

In May 2000, Aidan and his family visit Uncle George and Aunt Sunghee in New York.(1)(2) Aidan is (a) barely sentient and (b) unaware of their existence.

In July 2000, Uncle George and Aunt Sunghee attend Aiden's christening(3), the first of many rites that he will undergo as a member of the one true church.

In July 2001, Uncle George and Aunt Sunghee meet Aidan and his family in Buffalo at his paternal grandparents' home. The occasion is the celebration of Aidan's great-grandmother's 90th birthday.(4)A photograph taken on the occasion shows four generations of Greenslades.

In April 2002, Uncle George meets Aidan in Masschusetts. The occasion is the celebration of Harrison's christening.(5)

In July 2002, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George meet Aidan and his family in Buffalo at his paternal grandparents' home. (6)

In October 2002, Aidan and his family meet Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George in Westchester on the occasion of the entering into holy matrimony by Aidan's Uncle James and Aunt Stephanie.(7)

In February 2004, Aidan and his family meet many members of the Greenslade clan, including Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George, in Schenectady.(8)

In April 2004, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit Aidan and his family in Topsfield.(9)

In March 2005, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit the Greenslades in Topsfield.(10)

In April 2006, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit the Greenslades in Topsfield.(11)

In April 2009, Uncle George, Aunt Sunghee and George visit Aidan and his family in Topsfield.(12)

In September 2009, after attending the wedding of cousin Allison Duffy, the Greenslades of Topsfield and the Greenslades of New York City meet at Uncle Brian's house in New Jersey, along with many other relatives of AC on his paternal grandmother's side of the family.(13)

In December 2009, Aidan and his family meet Uncle George and his family and others in Myrtle Beach to celebrate the 50th wedding anniversary of Aidan's paternal grandparents.(14)

In February 2010, Aidan visits New York City with his siblings and paterfamilias. We stop in Economy Candy on Rivington Street(15) and the reader can be assured that Aidan and his siblings are very happy to have gone there.

In April 2011, Aidan visits New York City with his siblings and his paterfamilias.(16)

In March 2013, Uncle George visits the Greenslades in Topsfield. Aidan and Harrison take on Uncle George in a two-on-one tackle football game in the snow and are soundly defeated.

In May 2013, Uncle George visits the Greenslades in Topsfield. He and Aunt Jane take in a baseball game in which Aidan pitches.(17)

In August 2014, Uncle George visits the Greenslades in Topsfield. Uncle George visits the Avis builing at Logan Airport dedicated to Aidan's paterfamilias.(18)

In May 2015, Uncle George visits Topsfield.(19)

In November 2015, Aidan visits the Greenslades in New York City with Uncle Ken, cousin Colin and brother Harrison.(20)
